Our lab leverages a new understanding of the information contained
within bio and medical signals to create a more effective diagnostic tool
for imaging biological changes associated with diseases.
Success in this project brings effective new methods based on artificial intelligence (AI) to instruments enabling medical professionals to track the diseases as well as treatment responses.
Safe and low-cost methods can also be applied to patients frequently helping them directly observe the effects of lifestyle decisions.
